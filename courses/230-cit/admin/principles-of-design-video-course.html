Principles of Design

The fundamental principles of design are occassionally described using the acronym PARC. It represents Proximity, Alignment, Repetition and Contrast. These principles of design, along with Typography (the use of fonts) are important to web design in general and can have an impact on the User Experience and User Interface of any web page. You can also download or view a quick reference poster of these principles.
